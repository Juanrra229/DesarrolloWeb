LENGUAJES MAS UTILIZADOS

Although there is no exact figure, it is estimated that there are more than 200 programming languages in the world. There are those who raise the figure to 700. Many have fallen into oblivion. Others are eccentricities of their creators. And some lucky languages have allowed us to live in the hypertechnological society in which we are immersed. From the first punch cards to today's voice commands, thanks to programming languages it is possible to write in the language that machines understand.
